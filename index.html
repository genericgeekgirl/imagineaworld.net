Imagine a World

A TEXT ADVENTURE OF GIANT IMAGINATION

Imagine a World is an all-text re-imagining of Glitch. Glitch was a browser-based MMO that ran from September 2011 to December 2012. In December 2013, Tiny Speck released all of the artwork and some of the source code from the game under a CC0 license.

Imagine a World takes place in a paracosm called Ur. It was imagined by the eleven Giants, and was previously inhabited by a race called the Glitchen. You are a Tulpan, the first of a newly imagined race, tasked with restoring the world.
