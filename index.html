Imagine a Wrld

A TEXT ADVENTURE OF GIANT IMAGINATION

Imagine a World takes place in a paracosm called Ur. It was imagined by the eleven Giants, and was previously inhabited by a race called the Glitchen. You are a Tulpan, the first of a newly imagined race, tasked with strengthening the Giants' imagination and restoring the world.

Imagine a World is a text-based re-imagining of Glitch. Glitch was a wonderful browser-based MMO that ran from September 2011 to December 2012. In December 2013, Tiny Speck released the artwork and source code from the original game under a CC0 license.

I will be launching a Kickstarter soon. In the meantime, you can take a peek inside the Giants' imagination to view screenshots and learn more about the creative process. If you would like to help test the game, please sign up here. Beta-testers will receive a special in-game item!
